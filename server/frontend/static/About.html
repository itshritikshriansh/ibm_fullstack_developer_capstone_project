About Us

Welcome to Best Cars dealership, home to the best cars in North America. We deal in sale of domestic and imported cars at reasonable prices.

Rohan “The Visionary” Rastogi

Founder & CEO

Rohan once tried to sell his toy Hot Wheels car to a classmate in 2nd grade… with a warranty. He’s been hustling ever since. After founding the company in his garage (because of course), he declared war on boring cars and confusing salespeople. Now he spends his days “strategizing”—which mostly means drinking way too much coffee and calling meetings that somehow turn into TED Talks. Rohan believes in disruption, innovation, and that no car should ever be beige.

Fun Fact: He has a spreadsheet of every car he’s ever driven. It's color-coded. And scary.

Ankita “Spreadsheet Sorceress” Menon

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Ankita runs operations with the precision of a German-engineered gearbox. She once built an inventory tracking system using only Excel and sheer willpower. Her idea of fun is optimizing the vehicle dispatch workflow while listening to true crime podcasts. If a car’s late for delivery, she’ll know before the GPS does. Her favorite word is “scalability” and her least favorite is “winging it.”

Fun Fact: She labels her kitchen shelves—and her Uber drivers once asked if she worked for Google Maps.

Dev “Clicks & Gigs” Kapoor

Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Dev once created a viral TikTok ad featuring a talking hatchback. It got 2 million views—and sold exactly three cars. But hey, that’s branding! As the company's marketing guru, he lives in a world of hashtags, SEO sorcery, and slogans like “Drive Bold or Stay Home.” He believes every car has a story… especially the ones with fuzzy steering wheel covers. Dev is responsible for making sedans look sexy and SUVs sound adventurous.

Fun Fact: He A/B tests his Tinder bios. For market research. Allegedly.
